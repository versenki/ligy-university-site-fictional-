Courses Offered in our College of Computer Studies

Courtesy of Sean Anakin Coquia

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Focuses on algorithms, software engineering, and systems design. Students learn to build scalable applications and solve complex computing problems.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Requirements: Senior High School STEM strand or equivalent
  • Specializations: AI, Cybersecurity, Software Dev

Bachelor of Information Technology

Emphasizes practical IT skills including networking, database management, and web technologies for enterprise systems.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Requirements: STEM or ICT strand
  • Certifications: Cisco, AWS (optional)

Diploma in Web Development

Hands-on program covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React and Vue for front-end and back-end development.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Requirements: High school graduate
  • Portfolio: Required for graduation
