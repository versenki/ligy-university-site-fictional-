Courses Offered in our College of Business

Courtesy of Marcus V. Marcaida

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

Prepares students for leadership roles in marketing, finance, and operations through case studies and strategic planning.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Requirements: ABM strand or equivalent
  • Majors: Marketing, Finance, HRM

Diploma in Entrepreneurship

Designed for aspiring business owners, covering startup strategy, product development, and financial literacy.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Requirements: High school graduate
  • Capstone: Business pitch required

Certificate in Digital Marketing

Short course on SEO, social media strategy, and analytics tools for modern brand building.

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Requirements: Open to all
  • Tools: Google Ads, Meta Business Suite
