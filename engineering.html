Courses Offered in our College of Engineering

Courtesy of Daniel Gulla

Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering

Covers structural design, construction management, and environmental engineering with fieldwork and lab simulations.

  • Duration: 5 years
  • Requirements: STEM strand
  • Licensure: Required for practice

Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering

Focuses on embedded systems, signal processing, and telecommunications with hands-on circuit design.

  • Duration: 5 years
  • Requirements: STEM strand
  • Board Exam: Required

Diploma in Industrial Technology

Practical training in machine operations, safety protocols, and production systems for manufacturing industries.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Requirements: High school graduate
  • Certifications: TESDA NC II
