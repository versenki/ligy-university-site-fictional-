Courses Offered in our College of Education

Courtesy of Principal Dr. Timothy Stephen M. Lontac

Bachelor of Secondary Education

Prepares future educators in core subjects like English, Math, and Science with classroom immersion and pedagogy training.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Requirements: HUMSS or STEM strand
  • Licensure: LET required

Diploma in Early Childhood Education

Focuses on child development, play-based learning, and inclusive education for preschool and kindergarten levels.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Requirements: High school graduate
  • Internship: Required

Certificate in Special Education

Short course on adaptive teaching strategies, behavioral support, and inclusive classroom design.

  • Duration: 1 year
  • Requirements: Open to educators
  • Focus: Autism, ADHD, learning disabilities
